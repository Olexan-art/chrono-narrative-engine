    The Academy of Motion Picture Arts and Sciences has announced its second group of presenters for the 98th Oscars, with Oscar winner Javier Bardem, past nominees Demi Moore and Kumail Nanjiani, multiple Emmy winner Maya Rudolph, Chris Evans of Captain America fame, and breakout star Chase Infiniti from One Battle After Another set to present at the Dolby Theatre on March 15. This announcement follows the Academy's strategy of revealing presenters in stages to build anticipation for the prestigious ceremony. The diverse lineup combines established Hollywood veterans with emerging talents, reflecting the broad appeal of the annual event that celebrates cinematic achievements across various genres and backgrounds. The Dolby Theatre in Hollywood will host the ceremony where these celebrities will assist in presenting the coveted golden statuette to deserving nominees in the film industry's most celebrated recognition of artistic excellence.

    Чому це важливо

    This announcement is significant as it ramps up excitement for the 98th Academy Awards, the film industry's most prestigious event, by showcasing a blend of Hollywood royalty and rising stars. The selection of presenters like Javier Bardem and Chris Evans alongside newcomers such as Chase Infiniti demonstrates the Academy's strategy to appeal to a broad demographic and boost viewership ratings. For the nominees, the presence of high-profile presenters elevates the prestige of the moment, while for the audience, it promises an engaging broadcast filled with recognizable personalities.

    Контекст та передісторія

    • •The Academy of Motion Picture Arts and Sciences has been organizing the Oscars since 1929, making it the oldest and most prestigious award ceremony in the film industry.
    • •The 98th Academy Awards are scheduled to take place on March 15 at the Dolby Theatre in Hollywood, California.
    • •Javier Bardem is a previous Oscar winner, taking home the Best Actor award for his role in 'No Country for Old Men' in 2008.
    • •Demi Moore is a veteran actress who has been nominated for an Oscar in the past and remains a significant figure in Hollywood.
    • •The Academy frequently announces presenters in waves or stages to maximize media coverage and maintain public interest leading up to the live broadcast.

    Що далі

    The Academy is expected to announce additional waves of presenters in the coming weeks to further build hype for the ceremony. On March 15, the event will commence at the Dolby Theatre, where these celebrities will present awards to the winners in various categories. Following the ceremony, analysts will review the viewership numbers to see if this diverse lineup of presenters successfully attracted a large audience.
